Gingify puts your idle browser tabs to sleep — freeing RAM without closing them.
Click any sleeping tab to wake it instantly.

PRIVACY & TRUST
Zero telemetry. No data ever leaves your browser. Tab URLs are stored locally in
session storage and cleared when you close Chrome. No accounts. No servers. No tracking.
GPL-3.0 open source — every line of code is on GitHub. Read it yourself.

WHY GINGIFY?
Chrome's built-in Memory Saver is passive. You can't force-sleep a specific tab, can't see
RAM usage per tab, and can't set per-domain rules. Gingify gives you that control.

FEATURES
• Sleep any tab manually with one click
• Set per-domain rules: always sleep Reddit after 5 min, never sleep Gmail
• Real-time RAM estimate per tab so you know what's eating memory
• Sleep All / Wake All with one click
• Sleep on minimize — when you minimize Chrome, idle tabs sleep automatically
• Dashboard showing total RAM saved
• Zero data collected — all settings stay in your browser

HOW IT WORKS
1. Open the Gingify popup from the toolbar
2. Click Sleep on any tab — it freezes and frees its RAM
3. Click the sleeping tab anytime — it reloads instantly

PRIVACY
Gingify has no servers. No accounts. Tab URLs are stored locally in session storage
(cleared when you close Chrome). Your rules sync via Chrome's own sync system.

Free and open source.
